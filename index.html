Meet our Ampawssadors®:

Ampawssadors® Ampawssadors are our four-legged VIPs (Very Important Pets). Ampawssadors are our fabulous furry friends who bring the Nutrified lifestyle to life! These tail-wagging trendsetters get an all-access pass to the world of natural pet wellness:

  • Sneak peeks and first dibs on new Nutrified goodies
  • Exclusive invites to paw-some events, including our famous "Cat Walk"
  • Chance to shine in professional photo shoots on the red carpet
  • Opportunity to be the star of Nutrified's social media
  • Help shape the future of pet wellness with their valuable feedback

Ampawssadors® Events

In 2023, we took Poland by storm with our Ampawssadors® Catwalk events! From the bustling streets of Warsaw to the historic charm of Krakow, and from the coastal beauty of Gdańsk to the cultural hub of Wrocław, our furry ambassadors strutted their stuff on red carpets across the country.

These events weren't just about glamour - they were a celebration of pet health, natural wellness, and the incredible bond between pets and their humans. Attendees got to meet our Ampawssadors®, learn about Nutrified products, and even participate in fun, pet-friendly activities.

Stay tuned for our upcoming events in 2024 - we can't wait to meet more amazing pets and pet parents across Poland and beyond!
